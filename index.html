TrailSound — Privacy Policy

A simple, privacy-first field recording diary.

TrailSound does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data Storage

All recordings, notes, and location data are stored locally on your device. TrailSound does not upload or transmit your data to any servers.

Location

Location access is used only to attach recordings to places on your personal map. Location data never leaves your device.

Microphone

Microphone access is required to record audio. Audio recordings are stored locally and are never shared by TrailSound.

Analytics & Tracking

TrailSound does not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party SDKs.

Third Parties

TrailSound does not share data with third parties.

Changes

If this privacy policy changes, it will be updated on this page.
