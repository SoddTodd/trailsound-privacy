## Privacy Policy TrailSound does not collect, store, or share any personal data. ### Data Storage All recordings, notes, and location data are stored locally on your device. TrailSound does not upload or transmit your data to any servers. ### Location Location access is used only to attach recordings to places on your personal map. Location data never leaves your device. ### Microphone Microphone access is required to record audio. Audio recordings are stored locally and are never shared. ### Analytics & Tracking TrailSound does not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or third-party SDKs. ### Third Parties TrailSound does not share data with third parties. ### Changes If this privacy policy changes, it will be updated on this page. ### Contact If you have questions about privacy, you can contact: **[artoushan@gmail.com](mailto:artoushan@gmail.com)** *Last updated: December 2025*
